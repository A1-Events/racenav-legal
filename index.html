A1 RaceNav — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 June 2026 · Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how A1 Events SRL ("we", "us", "A1 Events") collects, uses, and shares information through the A1 RaceNav mobile application (the "App"). A1 RaceNav provides real-time GPS tracking and emergency SOS for participants in endurance racing events.

Who this applies to

A1 RaceNav is intended for registered participants of races organised or serviced by A1 Events and its partner organisers. You typically join an event using an invite code provided by the organiser.

Information we collect

We only collect what is needed to run the service:

We do not collect advertising identifiers, we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites, and we do not sell your personal data.

How we use your information

  • Provide live tracking and the event map to race organisers and safety staff.
  • Enable the emergency SOS feature so rescue services can locate you.
  • Manage your event participation.
  • Maintain reliability and security (diagnostics, crash reporting).

Background location

A1 RaceNav collects location in the background so that, during a race, your position remains visible to race control and SOS works even when the App is not open. Background collection runs only while you enable tracking and stops when you disable it or the event ends. You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings; the App's safety features will not function without it.

Sharing

During the event, the organizer and the organizer's designated staff (race control and the safety, sweep, and medical personnel acting on the organizer's behalf) can see your live position on the event map.

We share information only with:

  • Race organisers and safety/rescue personnel for the event you join — your location, name, and bib so they can manage the race and respond to emergencies.
  • Service providers that operate the App on our behalf: map rendering (Google Maps SDK), error/crash diagnostics (Sentry), and our hosting infrastructure. They process data only to provide their service to us.
  • Authorities or emergency services when necessary to respond to a life-safety situation or as required by law.

Data retention

Your GPS tracks, check-in records, and race data are retained indefinitely as part of the event archive — we use them for post-incident review, race-safety analysis, and historical records of the endurance events we organise. You can request deletion of your personal track data at any time under your GDPR right of erasure (see "Deleting your data" below). Diagnostic data is retained for a limited period by our diagnostics provider.

Deleting your data

A1 RaceNav does not use personal accounts — you join an event with a registration code provided by your organiser. To have your participation data and device records removed, contact your event organiser or email us at sibiu@a1events.ro and we will delete it.

Children

A1 RaceNav is not directed to children. If a participant is a minor, a parent or guardian should consent to location collection.

Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and standard safeguards to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, communicated in-app.
